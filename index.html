Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure? +

Yes, your data is completely secure. We don't store any of your emails or personal information on our servers. The app only accesses your emails through Google's secure API and all processing happens on your device.

Does EazyMail require internet access? +

Yes, EazyMail requires internet access to fetch your emails through the Gmail API. However, once emails are loaded, many features can work offline.

Can I use EazyMail with multiple Gmail accounts? +

Yes, EazyMail supports multiple Gmail accounts. You can easily switch between accounts within the app.
