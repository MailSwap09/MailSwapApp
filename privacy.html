Privacy Policy

Learn how EazyMail protects your privacy and handles your data.

Introduction

Welcome to EazyMail's Privacy Policy. Your privacy is critically important to us. EazyMail is designed with your privacy as a fundamental principle.

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your information when you use our application that interacts with Gmail through the Gmail API. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully.

Our Core Privacy Principle: EazyMail does not store, collect, or retain any of your emails or personal data on our servers. All processing occurs locally on your device.

Information We Access

To provide our service, EazyMail needs access to certain information from your Gmail account through the Gmail API, including:

  • Email content
  • Email metadata (sender, recipient, date, subject)
  • Labels and folders

How We Use Information

We use the information we access solely to:

  • Display your emails in our interface
  • Apply our organizational and visualization features
  • Provide custom views and styles for your email content

Data Storage and Retention

EazyMail does not store any of your emails or personal information on our servers. All data access and processing happen locally on your device. We do not:

  • Store copies of your emails on our servers
  • Retain any personal information after you close the application
  • Create databases of user information
  • Sell or share any user data with third parties

Google API Services User Data Policy

Our application adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. This means we only use the data accessed through the Gmail API for the purposes described in this policy.

Email Content Viewing

EazyMail only accesses your email content when you specifically request to view it. We do not:

  • Pre-load or batch download email content
  • Scan email content for advertising purposes
  • Use email content for purposes other than display and organization

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information when it is being processed on your device. These measures include:

  • Secure authentication with Google's OAuth 2.0
  • Local-only processing of email content
  • No persistent storage of credentials or email content

Third-Party Services

EazyMail does not share any information with third-party services. The only connection our application makes is directly to Gmail through the official Gmail API.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date below.
